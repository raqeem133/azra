Who We Are

Azra is a science and technology solutions company delivering mission-critical services and products to customers in the defense, intelligence, homeland security, civil, and health markets.

Our Mission

To make the world smarter through innovative science and technology solutions that address our customers' most critical challenges.

Leadership

Our leadership team brings decades of experience in science, technology, engineering, and systems integration.

Responsibility & Sustainability

We are committed to operating sustainably and responsibly, creating value for all our stakeholders while minimizing our environmental impact.

Ethics & Business Integrity

Ethical conduct is fundamental to our culture and operations. We maintain the highest standards of business integrity.

Partners

We work with leading technology partners to deliver innovative solutions to our customers.

Contract Vehicles

Our contract vehicles enable us to serve government and commercial customers efficiently and effectively.

Global Operations

With operations around the world, we serve customers globally while maintaining local expertise and responsiveness.

Our History

Founded in 1969, Azra has grown to become a leading provider of science and technology solutions for the world's most demanding customers.
